Problem / Opportunity
India is running out of clean water. 600 million people face extreme water stress. Millions of households depend on private water tankers every day — yet the experience is completely broken.

No Discoverability

Customers rely on word-of-mouth or pamphlets. No platform to search, compare, or book.

Opaque Pricing

Prices vary wildly. Suppliers exploit scarcity. Zero transparency or standardisation.

No ETA, No Tracking

After calling, customers just wait. No confirmation, no ETA, no live tracking. 30 min to 3 hours late.

No Quality Assurance

No verification of water quality, vehicle hygiene, or FSSAI compliance. Health risks unchecked.

Value Proposition
JalSeva is the first full-stack marketplace for water delivery in India — connecting customers with verified tanker suppliers through a voice-first, AI-powered PWA that works for users with low literacy.

Voice-First Ordering

Say "20 litre RO paani chahiye" in Hindi, Tamil, or 20+ languages. AI extracts intent instantly.

Real-Time Tracking

Live GPS tracking on Google Maps. See the truck, get ETA, call the driver — like Uber for water.

Transparent Pricing

See base + delivery + surge breakdown before booking. No hidden charges. UPI, card & cash.

Verified Suppliers

Every supplier is ID-verified (Aadhaar + FSSAI + water quality). Admin approves before going live.

WhatsApp Ordering

No app download needed. Message JalSeva on WhatsApp — Gemini AI handles the conversation.

ONDC Integrated

Connected to India's Open Network. Paytm, PhonePe, Google can discover JalSeva suppliers.
